FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the consultation really free? +
Yes — the first consultation (up to 30 minutes) is completely free. We believe every MSME should have access to quality guidance without financial barriers. For ongoing support, implementation, or detailed project work, we offer affordable retainer packages.
How quickly will someone get back to me? +
We respond within 24 business hours to all form submissions. For WhatsApp queries during business hours (9 AM – 7 PM IST, Mon–Sat), responses are typically within 2–4 hours. Urgent queries are prioritised — please indicate urgency in your message.
Can you help with loan applications? +
Yes. We provide guidance on which loan product is right for your business, how to prepare documentation, which banks / NBFCs to approach, and how to structure your application for maximum approval chances. We do not charge for basic guidance. For complex cases involving CGTMSE, project loans, or working capital restructuring, we may offer a paid facilitation service.
I have no accounting background. Can you help me with GST and tax? +
Absolutely. We specialise in explaining complex tax and compliance requirements in simple, jargon-free language. Our team can walk you through GST registration, monthly filing, input tax credit claims, advance tax payments, and ITR filing — step by step. We can also connect you with verified local CAs for ongoing compliance if needed.
Do you cover all states across India? +
Yes — we serve MSMEs across all 28 states and 8 UTs of India. Central government schemes (CGTMSE, ECLGS, PMEGP, Startup India etc.) are national in scope. For state-specific schemes, we provide guidance relevant to your state's policies and portals.
I'm just starting out — is MSMEHub relevant for me? +
Yes — especially for first-time entrepreneurs! We can guide you on entity formation (proprietorship vs Pvt Ltd), Udyam registration, basic compliance setup, initial banking relationship, and how to access startup / MSME schemes. Getting the foundation right saves significant trouble later.
Is my business information kept confidential? +
Yes — strict confidentiality is maintained. Your business details, financial information, and queries are never shared with third parties without your explicit consent. Our team operates under confidentiality norms standard to financial and consulting services.
Can you help me list on GeM (Government e-Marketplace)? +
Yes. We assist with GeM seller registration, product/service listing, category selection, and bidding strategy. We also help ensure your Udyam registration is linked correctly for the MSE reservation benefits on GeM.
