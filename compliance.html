Compliance Requirements by Category

📅

GST Compliance

GSTR-1 — Outward Supplies
Due: 11th of following month (monthly filers)
GSTR-3B — Monthly Summary + Tax Payment
Due: 20th (Turnover >₹5Cr), 22nd/24th (others)
GSTR-9 — Annual Return
Due: 31st December of following FY
CMP-08 — Composition Quarterly Statement
Due: 18th of month following quarter
GSTR-4 — Composition Annual Return
Due: 30th April of following FY
E-way Bill — For goods movement > ₹50,000
Generate before dispatch at ewaybillgst.gov.in
E-Invoice — Mandatory above ₹5 Cr turnover
Generate at einvoice1.gst.gov.in
💰

Income Tax Compliance

Advance Tax Q1 (15%)
Due: 15th June
Advance Tax Q2 (45%)
Due: 15th September
Advance Tax Q3 (75%)
Due: 15th December
Advance Tax Q4 (100%)
Due: 15th March
ITR Filing — Non-Audit Cases
Due: 31st July
ITR Filing — Audit Cases (Tax Audit u/s 44AB)
Due: 31st October
Tax Audit Report (Form 3CA/3CB + 3CD)
Due: 30th September (for audit cases)
🔖

TDS / TCS Compliance

TDS Deposit (each month)
Due: 7th of following month (March: 30th April)
TDS Return Q1 — Form 24Q / 26Q
Due: 31st July
TDS Return Q2
Due: 31st October
TDS Return Q3
Due: 31st January
TDS Return Q4
Due: 31st May
Issue Form 16 (Salary) / 16A (Non-salary)
Due: Within 15 days of filing Q4 return
👷

Labour Law Compliance

EPF — Monthly Deposit + ECR Filing
Mandatory: 20+ employees; Due: 15th of month
ESI — Monthly Contribution
Applicable: 10+ employees; wages ≤ ₹21,000; Due: 15th
Professional Tax (State-wise)
Varies: Monthly / Quarterly / Annual per state
Minimum Wages
Revised semi-annually; maintain wage register
Gratuity — Liability Provisioning
Applicable: 10+ employees; 5 yrs continuous service
Bonus (Payment of Bonus Act)
Applicable: 20+ employees; 8.33–20% of basic; due before Nov
Maternity Benefit Act
Applicable: 10+ employees; 26 weeks paid leave
🌿

Environmental Compliance

Consent to Establish (CTE) — SPCB/PCC
Before setting up new manufacturing unit
Consent to Operate (CTO) — SPCB/PCC
Before commencing operations; annual/biennial renewal
Hazardous Waste Management
Annual return; manifest system for disposal; CPCB rules
E-Waste Management
Producers/importers of electronics: EPR registration on CPCB portal
Plastic Waste Management
EPR registration; ban on specified single-use plastics from July 2022
Water / Air Cess
For factories using water for industrial processes; assessed annually
🏢

ROC / MCA Compliance (Companies & LLPs)

Annual Return — MGT-7 (Companies)
Due: Within 60 days of AGM
Financial Statements — AOC-4
Due: Within 30 days of AGM
Annual General Meeting (AGM)
Due: By 30th September each year
Director KYC — DIR-3 KYC
Due: 30th September annually
LLP Annual Return — Form 11
Due: 30th May
LLP Statement of Accounts — Form 8
Due: 30th October
Commencement of Business — INC-20A
Within 180 days of incorporation (Pvt Ltd)
🏭

Factories Act Compliance

Factory Registration / License
10+ workers with power OR 20+ without power
Annual Return (Form 21)
Due: 15th January
Half-Yearly Return (Form 22)
Due: 15th July (Apr–Sep) and 15th January (Oct–Mar)
Working Hours — Maximum Limits
9 hrs/day, 48 hrs/week; OT max 50 hrs/quarter
Health & Safety Registers
Maintain Form 7 (Register of Workers), accident registers
💳

MSME-Specific Compliance

MSME Samadhaan — File Delayed Payment Complaints
Buyers must pay MSMEs within 45 days of delivery
TReDS Onboarding — For MSME Suppliers
Companies >₹500Cr must onboard on TReDS platform
Udyam Classification Review
Annually based on ITR turnover; auto-updated via IT portal
Half-Yearly Disclosure to MSME Buyers
Companies with MSME suppliers must disclose dues in financials (Sec 22 MSMED Act)
