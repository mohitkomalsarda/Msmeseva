Chapter 01

Social Media Marketing

India has 600+ million internet users. Social media is no longer optional for MSMEs — it's the most cost-effective channel to build brand awareness, attract customers, and retain them. Here's how to leverage each platform strategically.

💬

WhatsApp Business

The most powerful free MSME marketing tool. Set up a business profile with catalogue, auto-replies, labels, and broadcast lists.

  • Product catalogue — customers browse and enquire directly
  • Broadcast lists — send offers to 256 contacts/broadcast
  • Quick replies — save time on common queries
  • WhatsApp Business API for scale (via Wati, Interakt, AiSensy)
  • Status updates — daily product/offer posts reach all contacts
Setup WhatsApp Business →
📸

Instagram & Facebook

Visual storytelling for B2C businesses. Instagram Reels and Facebook ads offer precise targeting by location, age, interest, and behaviour.

  • Create a Business Page — professional credibility + insights
  • Reels — 30–60 sec product demos get organic reach
  • Meta Ads Manager — ₹200/day campaigns with precise targeting
  • Lead Generation Ads — collect phone/email directly on app
  • Shopping catalogue — sell directly via Instagram Shop
Meta Business Suite →
💼

LinkedIn for B2B

Essential for B2B MSMEs, exporters, and service businesses. Build thought leadership and connect directly with procurement heads and buyers.

  • Company Page — professional brand presence
  • Personal profile of owner — founders get 5–10x engagement
  • Post industry insights, case studies, behind-the-scenes
  • LinkedIn Sales Navigator — find and connect with decision-makers
  • InMail campaigns — direct message to targeted professionals
Create Company Page →
📍

Google My Business

Free local SEO tool. When someone searches "textile manufacturer in Surat" or "CA near me", GMB ensures your business appears in Google Maps and local results.

  • Claim and verify your GMB listing — completely free
  • Add photos, working hours, products, services
  • Collect and respond to Google Reviews — key for trust
  • Post weekly updates — offers, events, new products
  • Insights: see how many people called, visited, got directions
Setup Google Business →
▶️

YouTube

Second largest search engine in the world. YouTube videos rank on Google too — powerful for product demos, tutorials, and brand storytelling.

  • YouTube Shorts — 60-sec clips for organic discovery
  • Product demos, factory tours, customer testimonials
  • How-to content builds authority in your niche
  • YouTube Ads — skippable ads from ₹0.10 per view
  • SEO-optimised titles and descriptions for discoverability
🐦

Twitter/X & Other Platforms

Platform selection matters — focus energy on 2–3 channels where your audience actually is, rather than being mediocre on all.

  • Twitter/X — for thought leadership, news, B2B networking
  • Pinterest — for home decor, fashion, food, lifestyle products
  • Snapchat — for youth-oriented consumer brands
  • Indiamart / JustDial — B2B product discovery (non-social but digital)
  • Consistency over frequency — 3 quality posts/week > daily spam

Chapter 02

Website Creation

Your website is your 24×7 salesperson, credibility builder, and customer acquisition engine. Banks also check your website during loan appraisal — it signals professionalism. A basic website can be built for ₹3,000–₹15,000/year.

🏗️ Website Platforms

  • WordPress — Most flexible; 1,000s of themes; ₹3,000–₹8,000/year hosting
  • Wix — Drag and drop; beginner-friendly; free plan available
  • Shopify — Best for e-commerce; ₹1,994/month; powerful
  • Webflow — Professional design; good for service businesses
  • Squarespace — Beautiful templates; good for portfolios

🌐 Domain & Hosting

  • Domain (yourbusiness.com): ₹500–₹1,000/year on GoDaddy, BigRock, Namecheap
  • Shared Hosting: Hostinger ₹79–₹199/month; Bluehost ₹199–₹299/month
  • SSL Certificate: Free with most hosts (Let's Encrypt) — mandatory for trust
  • Business Email (name@yourbusiness.com): Google Workspace ₹125/month
  • .in domain costs less (₹300–₹500) and ranks better for India search

✅ Website Must-Haves

  • Clear homepage: what you do, who you serve, contact CTA
  • Product/service pages with photos and specifications
  • About Us — founder story, year established, certifications
  • Contact form + WhatsApp chat button
  • GST invoice download (for B2B clients)
  • Google Analytics integration — track visitors
  • Mobile responsive — 70%+ traffic is from mobile

📈 SEO Basics

  • Use keywords your customers search — "cotton fabric manufacturer Surat"
  • Google Search Console — free tool to track search performance
  • Write blog posts answering customer questions — builds authority
  • Get listed on JustDial, IndiaMART, Sulekha — free backlinks
  • Page speed — compress images, use fast hosting
💡 Minimum Viable Website in 1 Day Use Wix or Hostinger's website builder — pick a template, add your business name, 5–6 product photos, contact details, and WhatsApp button. That's enough to start. Cost: ₹3,000–₹5,000 for domain + hosting for 1 year.

Chapter 03

Import & Export

Exporting can 3–5x your revenue. India's MSME exports account for nearly 45% of total exports. With the right setup, even a ₹50 lakh turnover business can sell globally and access foreign markets at premium prices.

📋 Getting Export-Ready

  • Udyam Registration (MSME Certificate)
  • IEC Code from DGFT — ₹500, applied online at dgft.gov.in
  • AD Code registration with your bank (for customs)
  • RCMC (Registration cum Membership Certificate) from export promotion council
  • Open EEFC Account (Exchange Earner's Foreign Currency) for forex receipts

🏪 Where to Find Export Buyers

  • Alibaba.com — world's largest B2B marketplace; Gold Membership ₹1–2L/year
  • IndiaMART — Indian B2B; buyers from 200+ countries
  • Amazon Global Selling — sell directly to US, UK, EU consumers
  • India Trade Portal — free government portal (indiatradenow.com)
  • Trade Fairs — India International Trade Fair, sector-specific fairs
  • Export Promotion Councils — EEPC, APEDA, FIEO, TEXPROCIL etc.

💵 Export Finance & Benefits

  • Export Packing Credit (EPC) — pre-shipment working capital at ~7–8%
  • 3% Interest Subvention on export credit for MSMEs
  • ECGC insurance — protect against buyer default risk
  • IGST refund on exports — claim on shipping bill
  • RoDTEP scheme — remission of duties and taxes
  • Advance Authorisation Scheme — duty-free inputs for export production

📦 Key Export Documents

  • Commercial Invoice (in foreign currency)
  • Packing List
  • Bill of Lading (sea) / Airway Bill (air)
  • Certificate of Origin (from Chamber of Commerce)
  • Shipping Bill (filed at customs for clearance)
  • GST LUT (Letter of Undertaking — for zero-rated export)

🚢 Key INCOTERMS Explained

  • EXW (Ex-Works): Buyer picks up from your factory — you do nothing
  • FOB (Free on Board): You deliver to port, load ship — buyer pays freight
  • CIF (Cost, Insurance, Freight): You pay shipping + insurance to destination port
  • DDP (Delivered Duty Paid): You handle everything including customs at buyer's country — highest obligation

📥 Import — Key Points

  • IEC mandatory for all imports
  • Pay Basic Customs Duty + IGST + cess on import
  • IGST paid at customs is fully available as ITC
  • Import under Advance Authorisation for duty-free raw materials
  • EPCG scheme — import capital goods at 0% duty against export obligation
  • Use a CHA (Custom House Agent) for smooth clearance

Chapter 05

Manpower & Hiring

Hiring the right people is critical for MSME growth. Here's a comprehensive guide to finding, hiring, and retaining talent across white-collar and blue-collar roles.

💼

White-Collar / Professional Hiring

  • Naukri.com — India's #1 job portal; post jobs from ₹1,500
  • LinkedIn Jobs — Best for management, sales, and tech roles
  • Indeed India — High volume; free basic posting
  • Internshala — Fresh graduates and interns (very affordable)
  • Hirect — Direct chat with candidates; no agency needed
🔧

Blue-Collar / Skilled Worker Hiring

  • Apna.co — India's largest blue-collar hiring app; free posting
  • WorkIndia — Factory workers, delivery, field sales
  • Betterplace — Blue-collar workforce management + hiring
  • Local ITI / Polytechnic colleges — Source trained technicians directly
  • District Employment Exchanges — Free government placement portal
🎓

Government Skilling & Apprenticeship

  • Apprenticeship India Portal (apprenticeshipindia.gov.in) — Hire apprentices at ₹5,000–₹9,000/month stipend; 25–50% reimbursed by govt
  • PMKVY (PM Kaushal Vikas Yojana) — Hire skilled youth with industry-relevant certification; training cost borne by govt
  • NCS Portal (ncs.gov.in) — National Career Service; free job posting for employers
  • NSDC — National Skill Development Corporation trained candidates sector-wise
Apprenticeship Portal →
🤝

Contractual & Flexi Staffing

  • TeamLease — India's largest staffing company; deploy quickly
  • Quess Corp — Flexi staffing, payroll management, compliance
  • Randstad India — White + blue collar contract staffing
  • Contractual hiring advantage: No PF/ESI liability if below thresholds; try before permanent hire
  • Contract-to-hire: standard 3–6 month trial before permanent
💡

Retention & Employee Engagement

  • Competitive salary benchmarked against market (use AmbitionBox, Glassdoor)
  • Timely salary payment — non-negotiable for retention
  • Clear growth path — people leave when they can't see a future
  • Recognition — simple monthly shoutouts cost nothing but mean a lot
  • ESOP / profit sharing for senior roles in growing companies
  • Regular 1-on-1 meetings — understand grievances early
📝

Hiring Best Practices

  • Write clear job descriptions — vague JDs attract wrong candidates
  • Structured interview — same questions for all candidates; compare fairly
  • Practical test / work sample before offer — reduces mis-hires
  • Reference check — call previous employer before final offer
  • Offer letter with clear terms — salary, notice period, role, KRAs
  • 90-day onboarding plan — new hires who are well-onboarded stay longer

Chapter 06

Lead Generation

A consistent pipeline of qualified leads is the lifeline of any growing MSME. Here's how to build a multi-channel lead generation engine that works 24×7.

🏪

B2B Marketplace Listings

List on platforms where buyers actively search for suppliers. These platforms have millions of verified buyers.

  • IndiaMART — 12 crore+ buyers; paid listings from ₹3,000/month
  • TradeIndia — Good for SME-to-SME; lower cost than IndiaMART
  • JustDial — Local and national B2B/B2C; ₹5,000–₹20,000/year
  • Sulekha — Services sector leads; pay-per-lead model available
  • ExportersIndia — For exporters looking for international buyers
🔍

Google Ads (Search & Display)

Appear when potential customers search for exactly what you sell. Highly targeted — you pay only when someone clicks.

  • Search Ads — appear in Google results for your keywords
  • Target by location (city/pin code), device, time of day
  • Budget: Start with ₹300–₹500/day; scale what works
  • Display Ads — banner ads on news sites, YouTube, apps
  • Remarketing — show ads to website visitors who didn't convert
📣

Meta Ads (Facebook & Instagram)

Best for B2C, local businesses, and awareness campaigns. Unmatched targeting capabilities — demographics, interests, lookalike audiences.

  • Lead Generation Ads — phone/email collected inside Facebook app
  • Lookalike Audience — target people similar to your existing customers
  • Retargeting — show ads to website visitors and video viewers
  • WhatsApp CTA Ads — click ad → chat directly on WhatsApp
  • Budget: ₹200–₹500/day; A/B test ad creatives
✉️

Email & WhatsApp Campaigns

Own your customer list — email and WhatsApp are channels you own, unlike social media which is algorithm-dependent.

  • Build email list: offer something valuable (guide, discount) in exchange
  • Mailchimp / Brevo — free up to 500 / 300 contacts/day
  • WhatsApp Broadcast — 256 contacts per broadcast (free via app)
  • WhatsApp API via Wati / Interakt — unlimited broadcasts to opted-in numbers
  • Personalised campaigns outperform generic bulk messages 3–5x
🤝

Referral & Channel Partners

Word-of-mouth and referrals are the highest-converting leads. Build a structured program to incentivise referrals.

  • Customer referral program — 5–10% discount / ₹500 credit per referral
  • Channel partners — appoint dealers/distributors who get 10–20% margin
  • Trade associations — FICCI, CII, local chamber events for networking
  • Complementary businesses — cross-refer each other (no-cost partnerships)
  • Case studies + testimonials — turn customers into brand ambassadors
📊

Track & Optimise Your Funnel

What gets measured gets improved. Set up basic tracking to know where your leads come from and what converts.

  • Google Analytics 4 — free; track website visitors, sources, conversions
  • UTM parameters — tag every link to know which campaign drove traffic
  • CRM pipeline — track every lead from enquiry to closed sale
  • Lead source tagging — always ask "how did you hear about us?"
  • Monthly review: Cost per lead, conversion rate, revenue per channel
